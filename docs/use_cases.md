# AI Chatbot Use Cases

## 1. Customer Support FAQ Bot

### Goal

Reduce repetitive support questions by answering common customer questions automatically.

### Example Questions

- How much is the shipping fee?
- Can I return a product?
- What time does the store open?
- Can you help me check my order?

## 2. Product Recommendation Assistant

### Goal

Help customers find suitable products based on needs and budget.

### Example Questions

- I need a product for oily skin under 300k.
- Can you suggest a moisturizer?
- Which product is suitable for beginners?

## 3. Internal FAQ Assistant

### Goal

Help staff search internal policy notes and business FAQ documents quickly.

### Example Questions

- What is the return process?
- How do I handle an order issue?
- What should I tell a customer about shipping?
